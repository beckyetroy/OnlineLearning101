Useful Apps and Resources


Nowadays, there are apps and online resources for just about anything, and online learning is no exception. To get the most out of your online learning experience, why not utilise what's out there to stay on top of your work?

Don't know where to start? We've got you! Below are some of our personal picks, tried and tested by us, to help you through your online learning experience. All of the below are FREE to download on PC or mobile:

Class Timetable is a simple but essential app for college students. It allows you to create a digital class timetable to have with you at all times on your mobile phone. Homework, project, and test details can also be stored and accessed conveniently in the 'Tasks' menu. The app also comes with a Widget and notifications for your phone.

1: Class Timetable App

Apple App store Download | Google Play store Download

Microsoft OneDrive is one of the handiest applications to have when learning online, or even in general. OneDrive allows you to upload all of your files from your computer or phone and stores it online to access at any time from any device. This means if you wanted to go to the library or anywhere to study, you could do so and access your notes without needing to bring your laptop or have all of your notes on your phone.

2: Microsoft OneDrive

Apple App store Download | Google Play store Download

Cam Scanner is a must-have app for anyone who hates typing or just prefers to write everything by hand. Cam Scanner allows you to take pictures of your handwritten notes or essays and instantly turn them into PDF files for your computer. This makes it quick and easy to upload your handwritten work.

3: CamScanner App

Apple App store Download | Google Play store Download

Grammarly acts similarly to a spell checker, with a few added bonuses. For starters, it can be added to any device or search engine (such as Chrome). It doesn't just check documents, but emails and online forums or tests too. It's also a lot faster than your average proof reader - perfect for time-based assessments that require typing!
